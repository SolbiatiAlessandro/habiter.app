Group Rules

We are a group of people serious about interview preparation, this group is about doing Builders together (100% free).

  • 1. You are added to a team of 2/3 people
  • 2. We run a daily practice at 9 PM
  • 3. There are 2 problems proposed. You solve one problem, you post a screenshot with the solution.
  • 4. At the end, you briefly explain you solution.
  • If not active for more then 2 days (no message sent), you are removed from the group.
