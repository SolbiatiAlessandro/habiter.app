The vision behind Habiter

Why we build Habiter

What's the problem we are trying to solve? I don't exercise as often as I want. I don't meditate as often as I would like. I would love to have someone that keeps me accountable, and is also going through the same process, so that we could share the experience in the meanwhile. We decided to build an app for collaborative habit tracking, where you join an online meetup of people working toward the same goal. It's like a offline meetup but it reduces all the friction of going to a offline event and dealing with the awkarness of the process. Let's fix this

Oana, building the product

Oana is a Youtuber excited about Start-Up world! A Software Engineer Intern at Facebook, now works full time on habiter. She is excited about getting stuff done and is energised by helping people finding their calling. With Habiter she wants to get people to really getting things done and finding communities they can relate to.

Alex, building the tech

Alex is a Software Engineer at Facebook by day and a Habiter.app Hacker by night. He loves going to offline meetups to get to know new people and cultivate his interests, but he ends up never going. With Habiter he wants to get the ball rolling for people in Tech that want to find a new co-founder, or find some buddy-developer to learn Javascript together.
